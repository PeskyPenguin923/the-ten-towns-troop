Session 1
NPCs Involved
Scramsax Danika Greysteel Mishana Hilin Trollbane Sephek
Summary

We saw Cinder fall from the sky bleeding from his chest. He came across an ornate sled with a man lying in it missing his chest. Zin then comes across Cinder and the sled. At this point Cinder blacks out from his wounds, and a Zin is attacked by something in the blizzard that touches him and puts him to sleep.

Cut to the inn we see Karnon coming downstairs and chatting with someone named Danika Greysteel, she says that she thinks Chwingas have something to do with the eternal winter. Gives Karnon a magical lamp and asks him to use it to find a chwinga for her.

Adar is led to the church to help heal some travelers that were hurt in the storm. Karnon heads the same way. The church sends Vera out to talk with Karnon and see why he's here. Karnon and Vera become friends :D. Adar heals Cinder before Zin wakes on their own.

Hilin Trollbane offers us 100 gold to kill Sephek A blue eyed man that's killing people who bribe for their names to be out of the offering. We learn that Sephek might be in East Haven and head that way. After 2 hours of travel by sled we see a caravan flying a flag with a wolfs paw on it. Karnon and Cinder approach the group, Cinder sneaks and Karnon provides the distraction. While Karnon is there he and Cinder over hear someone calling a guy with blue eyes Sephek. Cinder attempts to sneak back to the group after Karnon leaves and is heard. Sephek attacks the group and the rest of the caravan leaves. Sephek is eventually felled after a long battle, he went to renew his health and burned away.

We head back to town and see the sled with last nights offering on it, the girl is missing her chest. Some of the group is upset that we didn't attempt to save her. Hilin Pays us our gold and the session ends.
