The Ten Towns Troop
Introduction
The Ten Towns Troop is a group of travelers that have joined forces in the area known as Ten Towns. This tome will be used to record this groups stories and experiences.
Overview
There has been an everlasting winter in Icewind Dale, it is being caused by Auril. The adventures began by accepting quests in the town of Bryn Shandir.
